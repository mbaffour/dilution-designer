Welcome to Dilution Designer

A fast, flexible tool for designing any 96-well plate experiment — dose-response, inhibition, growth, fluorescence, and more. No account needed, runs entirely in your browser.

1Enter your compound / reagent name and stock concentration in the sidebar
2Add target concentrations one by one, or pick a preset dilution series
3Set replicates, toggle controls, pick fill order — the plate map updates live
4Click/drag wells to exclude rows, columns, or individual wells. Customize colors, export CSV, or print.
